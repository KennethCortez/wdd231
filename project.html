Site Name

BurnBright, Not Out

Reason: It is a play on words between shining brightly and burning out, and also refers to the purpose of the website, which is to provide tips on how to avoid burnout when coding.

Optional domain: Burnbright.dev

Site Purpose

It will be a website where i will give some advices and experiences about self-studying, give some testimonies of people that knows how hard it could be for someone to learn something on its own.

Scenarios

-Where do i have to go to start coding?

-How do i know if i am learning the right thing?

-How do i know if i am learning enough?
