Why is it important not to burn yourself out when learning?

Maintain Mental Health

Burnout can lead to stress, anxiety, and depression. Learning at a sustainable pace helps maintain mental well-being.

Enhance Learning Retention

Taking breaks and pacing yourself improves information retention and understanding, leading to better learning outcomes.

Foster Creativity

A balanced approach allows for creative thinking and problem-solving, essential skills in coding and development.

Build Sustainable Habits

Developing a routine that includes rest and self-care leads to long-term success in coding and personal growth.
